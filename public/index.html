NY Times Interview Maker

By Adnan Aga

Ever seen those moving, slow motion interviews from the NYTimes?

The ones that look like this?


I wanted to make my own, but ended up making a website so other people could make them too.

Make Your Own


Step 1

Record yourself using your phones slo-mo camera doing something against a grey/white wall. Make small movements. Be cute, be demure. Upload that recording below.

You can also record a video when you click the button below but then you can't do slo-mo like in the video up top.
